About Our College

Uniglobe Secondary School, located in Kathmandu, Nepal, offers +2 programs in Science, Management, and Law. Founded by experienced educators, the School aims to provide students with a relevant and up-to-date education that prepares them for success in the 21st century.

Uniglobe Secondary School believes in fostering a supportive and collaborative learning environment. Our dedicated faculty and staff work together to guide students not only academically but also in developing critical thinking and social responsibility. We are committed to excellence in education and innovation in learning.
